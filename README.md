# 📚 Love Letters: Immersive Digital Archive

> **"I am rooted, but I flow."** — *Virginia Woolf, The Waves*

An immersive, stream-of-consciousness digital sanctuary celebrating the legendary and passionate correspondence between modernist novelist **Virginia Woolf** and aristocratic poet **Vita Sackville-West**, spanning from 1922 until 1941. This project transforms literary history into a highly tactile, premium interactive experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📖 3D Tactile Vintage Book Mockup
- **Tactile Depth**: A realistic, 3D hardcover mockup featuring structured paper layers, rounded bookbinding, and a deep violet leather spine.
- **Dynamic Parallax**: The book shifts, tilts, and casts fluid organic shadows dynamically based on your cursor coordinates, providing a fully physical presence.

### 📜 Fixed Parchment Letters
- **Atmospheric Reading**: High-fidelity parchment overlay replicating aged letter paper (`parchment.png`) for distraction-free focus.
- **Friction-less Layout**: Custom-fit dimensions ensure parchment edges are preserved without internal overflow scrolls.

### 🎵 Ambient Audio Synthesis
- **Procedural Soundscape**: Integrated custom audio synthesis generating realistic parchment paper rustles, ink scratching, and keyboard typing sounds.

### 🎛️ Archive Dashboard & Configuration
- **Admin Control**: Toggle dashboard controls to configure typography sizes, line heights, ink colors, and add new archive letters dynamically.
